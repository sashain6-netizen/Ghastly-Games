◈ THE CHRONICLES OF ASH ◈

I. THE SUNDERING

Three hundred years ago, the kingdom of Vaelthorn stood as the crown jewel of the known world. Its spires of white stone caught the dawn, its people sang at harvest, and its king — Malachar the Radiant — ruled with a just and iron hand.

Then came the Hollow Night. In a single breathless moment, every flame in Vaelthorn died. Not snuffed, not guttered — simply... gone. As if fire itself had been unmade. When dawn never followed, the survivors called it the Sundering.

II. THE HOLLOW KING

King Malachar did not die in the Sundering. He was transformed. The darkness that took Vaelthorn poured into him, filling every hollow of his being with something ancient and wrong. His throne fused with his bones. His crown became his skull.

Now he sits in the dead capital, ruling over silence and ash, sending his Echoes — fragments of his shattered will given terrible form — to collect what sustains him: the last living memories of his former people.

III. THE ASHWALKERS

For generations, warriors called Ashwalkers have made the pilgrimage to Vaelthorn. Armed with Ember Blades — swords that hold a tiny stolen fragment of the world's last fire — they seek to end the Hollow King's reign.

None have returned.

You are KAEL, the last Ashwalker trained by the dying order. Your mentor gave you her Ember Blade with her last breath. You have nothing left to lose — which makes you more dangerous than all who came before.

IV. THE EMBER BLADE

Your weapon is not merely steel. The Ember Blade drinks the essence of what you defeat, growing stronger with each Echo destroyed. The flame within it flickers — barely alive. But like you, it endures.
